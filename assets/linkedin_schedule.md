# LinkedIn Posting Schedule

## Recommended Posting Times (IST - India Standard Time)

| Post | Day | Time (IST) | Time (PST) | Post Type |
|------|-----|------------|------------|-----------|
| 1 | Tuesday | 10:00 AM | 9:30 PM (Mon) | Short teaser |
| 2 | Thursday | 2:00 PM | 1:30 AM | Medium announcement |
| 3 | Saturday | 11:00 AM | 10:30 PM (Fri) | Long detailed post |

## Why These Times?

- **Tuesday 10 AM IST**: Catches US West Coast evening (Monday) + India morning
- **Thursday 2 PM IST**: Peak LinkedIn engagement for India + catches US waking up
- **Saturday 11 AM IST**: Weekend readers + Friday evening US audience

## Post Order Strategy

1. **Post 1 (Short)**: Build curiosity, get initial engagement
2. **Post 2 (Medium)**: Provide more details, include key features
3. **Post 3 (Long)**: Full story with tech details for deep engagement

## Engagement Tips

- Reply to EVERY comment within 1 hour
- Like comments immediately
- Ask follow-up questions to commenters
- Tag relevant people/companies (sparingly)
- Share in relevant LinkedIn groups after posting

## Hashtag Strategy

**Always include:**
- #AI
- #OpenSource
- #Python

**Rotate these:**
- #VoiceAssistant #Privacy #Whisper #Ollama
- #MachineLearning #SoftwareEngineering #Developer
- #BuildInPublic #SideProject #Tech

## Files to Use

- Short post: assets/linkedin_post_short.txt

## After Posting

1. Share link in relevant Discord/Slack communities
2. Post on Reddit (r/Python, r/MachineLearning, r/LocalLLaMA)
3. Tweet thread version
4. Update GitHub README with "Featured on LinkedIn" if engagement is high
